Our Services

24 Hours Emergency

Our 24-hour Emergency Department is dedicated to providing immediate and comprehensive medical care around the clock. Equipped with state-of-the-art technology and staffed by highly trained medical professionals, we handle all types of emergencies with precision and urgency. From trauma and critical care to minor injuries and sudden illnesses, we are here to offer rapid and effective treatment. Trust us to deliver expert care when you need it most, any time of day or night.

Trauma Care

Our Trauma Care Unit is equipped to handle severe and life-threatening injuries with the utmost expertise and speed. Staffed by a specialized team of surgeons, nurses, and support personnel, we provide immediate assessment and treatment for critical conditions. Utilizing advanced technology and protocols, we ensure that patients receive the highest standard of care during emergency situations. Our focus is on stabilizing, treating, and supporting patients through their recovery.

Urgent Care

Our Urgent Care Center offers prompt medical attention for non-life-threatening conditions without the need for an appointment. Open extended hours, we provide services for minor injuries, illnesses, and other urgent health concerns. Staffed by experienced healthcare professionals, we ensure quick, efficient, and compassionate care. Trust us for your immediate healthcare needs when your primary doctor is unavailable.
